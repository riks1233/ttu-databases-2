	NUMBERS:
		* smallint
		* integer
		* bigint
	CHARACTERS:
		* character
		* varchar (specified limit)
		* text (unlimited)

		* real
		* double precision (not so used, too heavy)
		* decimal and numeric (equal things) [decimal(täpsus, skaala) täpsus - üldine numbrite arv, skaala - palju peale koma.]
